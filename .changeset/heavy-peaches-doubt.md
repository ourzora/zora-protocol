Add agent trading skills: copy-trader, early-buyer, watchlist, and take-profit

- `/copy-trader` mirrors another user's portfolio with options for existing holdings, future trades, or both
- `/early-buyer` monitors creators for new coin launches and auto-buys
- `/watchlist` tracks coins and alerts on market cap thresholds
- `/take-profit` auto-sells at configurable take-profit and stop-loss targets
